Privacy Policy

How this website handles information.

NEXAI uses this website as a corporate communications channel for customers, partners, and visitors evaluating the company. This policy explains what information may be collected through that process, how it is used, and where to direct privacy-related requests. Effective March 8, 2026.

Information you provide

If you contact NEXAI through the website's email links or message handoff, you may provide contact details such as your name, email address, organisation, and the contents of your enquiry. We use that information to respond, route your message to the relevant team, and maintain a record of inbound communications when appropriate.

Technical information

When you visit the site, standard technical data may be processed to deliver the pages securely and reliably. This can include IP address, browser and device details, requested pages, timestamps, and basic server or network log information.

How information is used

  • To respond to commercial, partnership, legal, or general company enquiries.
  • To operate, secure, and improve the website and related communications.
  • To maintain records of inbound requests and the company's responses.

Third-party services

The website may rely on third-party infrastructure to deliver pages, load web assets, and open external email applications. Those providers may receive the technical request data needed to serve content or complete the action you initiate.

Sharing, retention, and security

Information submitted through the site may be shared internally with teams responsible for commercial, partnership, legal, or general follow-up, and with service providers that support hosting or communications. We retain information only as long as reasonably needed for operational, compliance, or record-keeping purposes, and we apply reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect it.

Your choices

You can choose not to use the website's contact routes and instead reach NEXAI through other published channels. For privacy questions, data requests, or concerns about how website information is handled, contact info@nexai.id.

For company, commercial, or legal questions, use the contact routes published on the homepage so your enquiry reaches the right team quickly.
