Privacy Policy

How this website handles information.

This policy describes the types of information the NEXAI website may collect, how that information is used, and how you can contact us with privacy-related questions. Effective March 8, 2026.

Information you provide

If you contact NEXAI using the website's email actions or contact form handoff, you may provide your name, email address, company name, and the contents of your message. That information is used to respond to your enquiry and route it to the appropriate team.

Technical information

Like most websites, this site may process technical information such as IP address, browser details, device type, timestamps, and requested URLs through normal server logs and content delivery mechanisms.

How information is used

  • To respond to commercial, partnership, legal, or investor enquiries.
  • To operate, secure, and improve the website and related communications.
  • To maintain records of inbound requests and the company's responses.

Third-party services

The website may request third-party resources such as hosted web fonts or external email applications. Those providers may receive technical request data as part of delivering their services.

Sharing, retention, and security

Information may be shared internally with relevant teams or with service providers supporting website operation and communications. Information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary for operational, legal, or record-keeping purposes. NEXAI uses reasonable administrative and technical safeguards, but no internet transmission is completely secure.

Your choices

You may choose not to send information through the website. To ask privacy-related questions or request assistance, contact info@nexai.id.

If your enquiry relates to investor materials or public-company disclosures, use the investor-relations contact path so your request is routed appropriately.
